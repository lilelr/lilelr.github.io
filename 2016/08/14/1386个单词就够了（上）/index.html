1386个单词就够了（上）

发表于 2016-08-14

##上篇 为什么只需要1386个单词

###Skill one 挑个大的
上义词 下义词

####get 能有108个下义词
get -> inherit
P91
I get a lot of E-mails every day. (receive)
The policeman finally got him. (catch)
I’ll get the doctor. (call）
Let’s get some fresh air. (breathe)
She gets $19 an hour. (earn)
He didn’t get the joke. (understand)
What time will we get there? （arrive)

####have, give, take, make

####think 想-> doubt, suspect 怀疑
consider,suppose,guess,believe,anticipate,judge,ponder,contemplate

####有感觉的都是feeling 无论肉体上的感受，还是情感上的感受
I didn’t mean to hurt your feelings.
You have no feelings for others. (love)
I got the feeling that he didn’t like me. (impression)
I didn’t have strong feeling about it.(opinion)
I’ve lost all feeling in my legs.(sense)

####bad不都是“坏”的意思
I thought it was a very bad article. (inferior)
Smoking gives you bad breathe. (unpleasant)
He had a bad accident. (serious)
I afraid I’m feeling pretty bad. (ill)
She felt bad about leaving him. (guilty)

###Skill two 挑个实心的
It is an important(空心的，抽象的） decision.
It is a big（实心的，具体的） decision.
空心->实心 隐喻

####size不光是尺寸的意思
Nobody realized the size of the problem.
She knows the size of the task.
We were shocked at the size of his debts.

###Skill three 捏软乎的

####正着不会说反着说

####keep -> “可以留着“
You can keep the change.
Keep it to yourself.
He keeps something from his wife.

####off 否定作用
All the shirt are 20% off.
How much do you want your hair off?
His free throw shooting was off.
He rides bicycle with his hands off the handle.

####我不会说，你来说
Her youngest son got all her property.
She gave all her property to her youngest son.

####人会说，东西说
P265

####以物作主语的常见动词， go,come,bring,say,speak,wait,stay,stop
All her property goes her youngest son.
The award goes to X.
I don’t know how my money goes.
What brings you here today?
The paperwork can stay till tomorrow.
Her success speaks for itself.
What do these figures say?
Your responsibility doesn’t stop here.

###Skill Four 自产自用
P360
How often does earthquake happen in Japan? (frequency)
Getting married is more important than other things and should be done first. (priority)

#####形容词（描述事物在某一方面的性质或状态） 裸体、慢性病
She wears nothing. （naked)
This disease will last for a long time. (chronic)

######解释说明
glare -> to look at somebody angrily
shout -> to speak loudly to somebody

###三种典型的英语思维
P552

  • it 解决主语缺少问题
  • take
  • make

####差别
P562
There is a car for you if you get the job.
A car goes with this job.
I was late when I went to word yesterday.
I was late for work yesterday.

  • 汉语习惯以人作主语，英语经常用物作主语
  • 汉语强调动作，英语强调方位
  • 汉语中动词在英语中经常被介词来代替；
  • 英语的语序是先说主谓宾，再说其他，而汉语常常把其他成分放在谓语前
  • 英语口语中形容词汇用得比较多

return -> give back
Throw the ball into the basket.
Throw the ball through the basket. (篮筐是空心的，球进去会出来）
